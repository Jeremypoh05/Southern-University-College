Programmes We Offer

We will offer more programmes imminent, follow us for get more information !

Foundation
Programmes

Foundation is a one-year duration programme after high school and before university. This programme will prepare you for an undergraduate degree at our university.

Diploama
Programmes

A Diploma course is a level below an undergraduate degree and can be considered equivalent to a first-year Degree at university. Typically, you need to take 24-36 months to complete.

Undergraduate Programmes

This programme also called undergraduate degree. We offer associate's degree and bachelor's degree which need to take at least three or four years to complete.

Post-Graduate Programmes

If you have successfully completed an undergraduate degree level course at our university, you may choose this program and are undertaking further study at a more advanced level.
