To avoid delays in the process, please ensure all information required in the application is complete and correct.

Malaysia Students


Please include the following :


  • 1 recent passport sized photograph
  • Your Mycard (IC)
  • Certified true copy of school leaving/completion certificate
  • Certified true copies of academic qualifications
  • Enrolment Fee of RM500.00 (non-refundable)*
  • Hostel Application Fee of RM500.00 (if required)
  • Cucuriculum Certificate

Please note that your application will not be processed if the
above documents are not submitted.

International Students


Please include the following :


Step 1: Submit documents to verify entry eligibility to us :
  • 1x Certified High School Result / College Result
  • 1x Certified School Leaving Certificate / Graduation Certificate
  • 1x IELTS Test Report Form
Step 2: After approved, Fill up Registration Form together
with the following additional documents;)
  • Student Residence Application Form (If Application)
  • 1x Photocopy of passport (All pages including blank pages)
  • 10x Passport-size photograph with WHITE BACKGROUND (indicating your name and passport number at the back)
  • 1x Pre-Medical Report (Obtainable from ISU)
  • RM3500 Application and Registration Fees
Step 3: Once all registration procedures are approved, the student is required to make first year’s Tuition Fees and start his/ Her study.

Please note that your application will not be processed if the
above documents are not submitted.
